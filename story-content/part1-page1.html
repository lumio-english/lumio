Part 1 of 4 • The Mysterious Map

One morning, Lumi said, "Good morning!" to everyone in the classroom. While looking for a book, Lumi found something shiny hidden between the pages — a magic map! "Hi, friends! Look what I found!" said Lumi. Sara, Omar, and Noor ran over to see — and gasped!
